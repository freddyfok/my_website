Wizard Card Game

Wizard is a card game played with a standard size 52-card deck plus 4 Wizards and 4 Jesters cards.
Before each round, you bet how many tricks you will win in that round.
To gain points, you need to win the amount you bet.
You will lose points for winning over or under the amount you bet.
